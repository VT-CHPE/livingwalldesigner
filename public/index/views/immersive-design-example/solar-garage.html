The Solar Garage

In-Depth Exploration

These images are of the full scale construction of the Solar Garage at the Research nad Demonstration Facility in Blacksburg, Virginia. It is the combination of learning adn understanding structure and how vegetated assemblies could be applied to an experimental frame. The images use a mix of photos, watercolor, prisma markers, colored pencils, and graphite.

The project was used to verify the expanded framework, among other methods.
